## Ideas (en vrac):
-# All the unorganized ideas that I have for a first complete and fun version (around 10 hours of game):

General purpose of this mod :
Having fun through "the lore of (two -> four) civilizations which were overpowered and fought gigantic battles annihilating themselves."
The mystery = "But for what purpose were they fighting ?"
Next part of the lore = "The Chaos Master told the 2/4 civilians to fight for the cube of chaos because if another found it, they will destroy the world.
So in order to preserve the peace, they had to find it first. And stop others from destroying the world"
The Goal = "Kill the guardians to awaken the Boss of the Chaos and kill him to save the honor of the past civilians"
Conclusion = "To much ambition of power leads to annihilation"
